Amazon Scraper API

Usage

1. Search

/search/ProductName ... In place of Product name you can search anything available on Amazon MarketPlace e.g. (Smartphones, laptops, applications etc.)



2. Product Details

/product/ProductID ... In place of ProductID you can place product ID of anything available on amazon to display it's details



3. Product Reviews

/product/ProductID/review ... In place of ProductID you can place product ID and add /reviews to display all reviews available on that product



4. Product Offers

/product/ProductID/offers ... In place of ProductID you can place product ID and add /offers to display all offers available on that product
