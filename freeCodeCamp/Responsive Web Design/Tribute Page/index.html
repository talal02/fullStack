Imran Khan

1992 Cricket World Cup Winner for Pakistan 🇵🇰

Here's timeline of Imran Khan's Life:
  • 1952 - Born in Lahore, Punjab, Pakistan
  • 1971 - Began his international cricket career at age 18, in a 1971 Test series against England.
  • 1975 - Graduated from England's Keble College in 1975.
  • 1994 - Founded first ever cancer hospital in Lahore that is mostly free for all.
  • 1996 - Founded the Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf (PTI) in 1996
  • 2002 - Won a seat in the National Assembly in 2002, and saw Khan serve as an opposition member from Mianwali until 2007.
  • 2005 - On 23 November 2005, He was appointed as the chancellor of University of Bradford, succeeding Baroness Lockwood.
  • 2014 - He stepped down as chancellor, citing his "increasing political commitments".
  • 2018 - At the conclusion of the count on 28 July, the Election Commission of Pakistan (ECP) announced that the PTI had won a total of 116 of the 270 seats contested. Khan became the first person in the history of Pakistan general elections who contested and won in all five constituencies.
  • 2018 - He became 22nd Prime Minister of Pakistan.
  • 2022 - On 10 April, a no-confidence vote was conducted and he was ousted from office, becoming the first prime minister in Pakistan to be removed from office by a vote of no confidence.
  • 2022 - It was a result of Khan's independent foreign policy, he was ousted as a result of foreign regime change by most corrupt politicians and Army Chief (Qamar Javed Bajwa).
